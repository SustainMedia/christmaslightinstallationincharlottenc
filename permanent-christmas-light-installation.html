What Are Permanent Christmas Lights?

Permanent Christmas lights are high-quality LED lights that stay on your home or business all year. They are:

Hidden from view when not in use

Durable and weather-resistant

Controllable with your smartphone

Available in multiple colors

Energy-efficient and long-lasting

Benefits of Permanent Christmas Lights

No More Yearly Installation

Say goodbye to climbing ladders every December. With permanent lights you can enjoy holiday displays without the annual hassle.

For homeowners

Turn on your holiday display with a simple tap on your phone. Save time during the busy holiday season and eliminate storage concerns for bulky light strands.

For businesses

Create a festive atmosphere year-round with lighting that adapts to any season or promotion. Impress clients and customers with professional lighting that shows attention to detail.

Your permanent lighting system works for all occasions throughout the year. Show your spirit with red white and blue for Fourth of July or team colors for big game days.
