What Are Permanent Christmas Lights?

Permanent Christmas lights are high-quality LED lights that stay on your home or business all year. They are:

Hidden from view when not in use

Durable and weather-resistant

Controllable with your smartphone

Available in multiple colors

Energy-efficient and long-lasting

Benefits of Permanent Christmas Lights

No More Yearly Installation

Say goodbye to climbing ladders every December. With permanent lights you can enjoy holiday displays without the annual hassle.

FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Answering your questions about our light installation services.

How much does professional Christmas light installation cost?

Our Christmas light installation costs depend on the size of your home and how many lights you want. Most of our residential jobs range from $250 to $1,200. We provide free estimates so you'll know exactly what you'll pay before we start. The price includes installation, maintenance during the season, and removal after the holidays.

Do you provide the lights and decorations, or do I need to have my own?

We provide all the lights and decorations you need! We use commercial-grade LED lights that are brighter and more reliable than store-bought lights. You can choose from different colors and styles. If you already have decorations you love, we can install those too.

What happens if some lights stop working during the holiday season?

Don't worry! We include free maintenance with every installation. If any lights stop working, just call us and we'll come fix them at no extra cost.

When should I book my Christmas light installation?

Book as early as possible! Our schedule fills up quickly starting in September. Most customers book in September or early October to get their preferred installation date. We install lights from early November through mid-December, but the earlier you book, the more options you'll have.

Do you take down the lights after the holidays, or is that something I need to do?

Yes, we take care of everything! Our service includes removal of all lights and decorations after the holiday season. Most takedowns happen in January, and we store everything carefully so nothing gets damaged. You don't have to lift a finger!

Are your installers trained and insured in case of accidents?

Absolutely! All our installers are fully trained, experienced, and insured. We carry liability insurance and workers' compensation insurance to protect you and your property. Safety is our top priority, and we use professional equipment and follow strict safety procedures when working on your home.
